About Me

Hello there! I'm Sebastian, currently in my second year of Computing Information Systems at the University of Greenwich. Originally from Argentina, I've called London home for the past five years, where I've been immersing myself in both the vibrant tech scene and the diverse cultural experiences this city offers.
I started this journey of learning programming during the COVID period, initially as a hobby. However, as I dug deeper, I discovered that I enjoyed it and that there aren't many things similar to the satisfaction that comes from being able to solve a problem you were stuck on for hours, which ultimately led me to want to pursue this.
I am set apart by my unwavering commitment to delivering quality work and ensuring tasks are completed to the best of my ability. Collaborating in team environments is something I enjoy, based on university work experiences where I have contributed effectively to group projects.
I am excited about the possibilities that technology offers and I would like to continue expanding my skills, which is why I am willing to learn about new technologies that the company requires.
